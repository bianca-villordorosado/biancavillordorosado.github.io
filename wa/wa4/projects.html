Projects:

Digital Legacy Clinic

The Digital Legacy Clinic is modeled on law schools that offer pro-bono legal support from students. The clinic helps manage digital accounts from loved ones who have passed away and helps clients create a plan for their own digital accounts. Learn more about my contribution to the Digital Legacy Clinic.

OnTrack

OnTrack is a job search app that was developed with university students in mind. OnTrack offers students a way to search for jobs using precise filters, apply to jobs faster and track completed job applications all in one app. Learn more about the OnTrack app.

Nature Navigators

Nature Navigators is an app that was developed with the goal of making learning about the local environment more engaging and accessible for hikers. The app allows users to learn more about plants they see with an AR feature.Learn more about the Nature Navigators app.
