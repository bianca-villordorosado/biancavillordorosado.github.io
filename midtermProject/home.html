Designing intuitive and meaningful digital experiences

I'm a User centered designer with a strong foundation in the design cycle, from research and wireframing to usability testing and iteration. Skilled in creating intuitive digital experiences through thoughtful visual designs that are accessible.

I’m driven by a passion for designing digital experiences that are both intuitive and meaningful. I believe technology should make life easier, more inclusive, and more engaging. That’s why I’m drawn to UX/UI design.

As an Information Science major with a minor in Creative Technology & Design at CU Boulder, I’m developing strong skills in user research, prototyping, and interaction design. I enjoy combining creativity with problem solving to bring ideas to life, and I’m always eager to learn new tools and use different approaches to achieve this.

One of my favorite projects was helping design CU Boulder’s Digital Legacy Clinic website, a resource that supports the public in navigating their digital assets. This experience gave me the chance to apply user centered design principles to a real world project that makes an impact by helping others.

I’m excited to continue growing as a designer and to contribute to projects that connect people with technology in thoughtful ways. You can explore the projects I've worked on the projects page.
